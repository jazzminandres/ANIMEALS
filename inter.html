Step by Step

Order in
4 Easy Steps

01

Create Your Account

Sign up using your DLSUD student email. Setup takes under a minute and gives you full access to all campus canteen menus.

02

Browse and Pre-Order

Explore available meals, customize your order, and choose your pickup time slot that fits between your classes.

About the Platform

Smarter Eating for DLSUD Students

ANIMEALS is a campus food pre-ordering platform built exclusively for De La Salle University Dasmarinas. Skip the canteen queue. Pre-order between classes and choose to pick up at the counter or have your meal delivered right to your building.

Designed with a soft neumorphic interface, ANIMEALS makes every order smooth, fast, and stress-free.
